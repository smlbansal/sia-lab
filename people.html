Principle Investigator

Somil Bansal

Somil joined as an assistant professor in the ECE Department at the University of Southern California in Fall 2021 after working on safety and planning for autonomous vehicles at Waymo. His long-term goal is to develop a theory that formalizes the design, analysis, and control of autonomous robots that operate intelligently but safely in complex environments.
