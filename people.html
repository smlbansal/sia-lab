Principle Investigator

Somil Bansal
Somil is an assistant professor in the AA Department at Stanford university. Before that, he was an assistant professor in the ECE department at the University of Southern California. His long-term goal is to develop a theory that formalizes the design, analysis, and control of autonomous robots that operate intelligently but safely in complex environments.
