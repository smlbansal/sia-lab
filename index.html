The Safe and Intelligent Autonomy Lab is part of the Ming Hsieh Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering in the USC Viterbi School of Engineering . We seek to design autonomous robotic systems that operate with guaranteed safety and performance in new settings and environments. Our research combines machine learning with classical, model-based planning and control methods in pursuit of this endeavour.

We are looking for undergraduate and graduate students to join our research group. If you are interested in robotics, control, and/or machine learning, consider joining us!
