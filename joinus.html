Join Us

We are looking for talented students and postdocs to join our lab. Please follow the instructions on this page before emailing me; that will make sure we don't have to email back and forth, and you will receive a much faster reply.

Prospective PhD students

Prospective graduate students are encouraged to apply to the ECE PhD program (the deadline is typically in mid-December). In your application, please mention my name under the faculties of interest. Additionally, I encourage you to reach out to me after the application has beed submitted. Please include your CV, brief description of your research interests, and any relevant application material in your email.

The students who are applying for/or have received fellowships are of particular interest. Please see the section below on some fellowship options for graduate students.

Please note that the admissions at USC are coordinated at the department level so, unfortunately, I would not be able to help with any admission-specific requests/questions. Please direct them to info@ee.usc.edu.

Prospective MS students

Please apply to the ECE MS program. I would love to chat more about potential research collaborations after you have been admitted.

Current PhD students

Please send me an email with the following information and I will get back to you as soon as possible.
  • CV
  • UG and graduate transcripts
  • A brief description of why you are interested in the lab, and which research projects in the lab interested you and why.
  • Experience and background (if any) in ML, control, and/or robotics.
  • Experience (if any) with training deep networks, ROS, and/or working with real hardware.
  • Please indicate whether you will need funding or not. The students who are applying for/or have received fellowships are of particular interest. Please see the section below on some fellowship options for graduate students.

Current UG/MS students

Please send me an email with the following information and I will get back to you as soon as possible.
  • CV
  • UG and graduate (if applicable) transcripts
  • A brief description of why you are interested in the lab, and which research projects in the lab interested you and why.
  • Experience and background (if any) in ML, control, and/or robotics.*
  • Experience (if any) with training deep networks, ROS, and/or working with real hardware.*
  • Please indicate whether you are looking for a funded or a non-funded/externally funded position.
*Note that experience in robotics/control/ML is not required, but the above information will help me in finding a better project for you.
